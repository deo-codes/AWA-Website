Welcome to AWA

Arkansas Wrestling Association (AWA) is a friendly family show based out in Blytheville, Arkansas. We run every Second Saturday of the month. We have a locker room full of veterans & young and upcoming talent. Founded By Mark, TW, and Luke Justice. Scroll below for more content

Our Services

Live Pro Wrestling Events

See your favorite wrestlers and legends in the business LIVE as we bring you awesome pro wrestling action in the state of Arkansas. See further updates to see when the next AWA show comes to your area in the Upcoming Shows and Event section below.

Birthdays/Fundrasiers

If you or your kids want to host a birthday party or a fundraiser for an organization, you come to the right place. We can host it to either one of our live events or in a backyard Just call us at either 870-278-9133 or 870-838-9991 for more information.

Training

Ever Wanted To Become a professional wrestler? Why Wait? You can start learning today. You will learn wrestling psychology, calling matches, promos, proper bumping, chain wrestling, and more! Just call us at either 870-278-9133 or 870-838-9991 for more information.
